About Jacob Brothers Painting

Family Owned & Operated Since 1985

Our Story

Jacob Brothers' Painting got started by Father and Son in 1985 in Columbus, Ohio. Mainly painting high-end homes in the exclusive Arlington and Bexley neighborhoods, we built our reputation on quality work and attention to detail.

Moving to Southern California has enabled the company to grow, and additionally do Commercial properties. We are a family-owned business and have been beautifying homes and businesses for over 20 years.

We do one job at a time and take the utmost pride in our work. Your satisfaction is our number one priority, and we treat every project as if it were our own home or business.

Our team consists of painters from Eastern Europe with 10+ years in old-fashioned restorative painting. We support the practices and guidelines of the PDCA (Painting and Decorating Contractors of America), ensuring the highest standards in our industry.
