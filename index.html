About Jacob Brothers Painting

Family Owned & Operated Since 1985

Jacob Brothers' Painting got started by Father and Son in 1985 in Columbus, Ohio. Mainly painting high-end homes in the exclusive Arlington and Bexley neighborhoods.

Moving to Southern California has enabled the company to grow, and additionally do Commercial properties. We are a family-owned business and have been beautifying homes and businesses for over 20 years.

We do one job at a time and take the utmost pride in our work. Your satisfaction is our number one priority.
