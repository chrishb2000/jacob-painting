General Questions

Why hire a professional painting contractor?

Before you decide to hire the least expensive painter you can find, or "do it yourself", here are important points to consider:

SAFETY - Painting can be hazardous work, whether it's working on a ladder or dealing with solvents and dust. Hiring a professional contractor means you don't have to worry about these problems.
HEALTH - If your home or business was built before 1978, it may have been painted with lead-based paint. Professional contractors will take appropriate steps to protect you and follow EPA recommendations.
PREPARATION & PAINTING - Getting the surface properly prepared makes all the difference. Professional contractors know the value of preparation and will do it right.
KNOWING PAINTS - The choices today are more complicated than just oil or latex, and professional contractors will know what works, how to use it, and will have the right tools for the job.

Are your estimates free?

Yes, all our estimates are completely free with no obligation.

We believe in providing transparent, honest pricing upfront so you can make an informed decision about your painting project.

What factors affect the cost of a painting project?

Several factors can affect the final cost:

  • Size of the area to be painted
  • Condition of surfaces (repairs needed)
  • Number of coats required
  • Type and quality of paint selected
  • Accessibility (height, obstacles)
  • Preparation work needed
  • Timeline requirements
Do you require a deposit?

For most residential projects, we do not require a large deposit. Payment terms are discussed during the estimate process and outlined in your written contract.

We believe in earning your trust through our work, and payment schedules are structured to protect both parties.

Process & Timeline

How many jobs do you work on at any given time?

We work on one job at a time.

This is one of the key differences that sets us apart. By focusing on one project at a time, we ensure:

  • Complete attention to your project
  • Faster completion time
  • Consistent crew presence
  • Direct owner supervision
  • Better communication

Each job is supervised by a family member with over 20 years of painting experience.

How long will my painting project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and conditions:

  • Interior room: 1-2 days
  • Whole house interior: 3-7 days
  • Exterior home: 5-10 days
  • Commercial projects: Varies by size

We'll provide a detailed timeline in your written estimate and keep you informed of any changes.

Will I need to be home during the painting?

For exterior projects, you don't need to be home as long as we have access to the property.

For interior projects, we prefer someone to be home for the initial walkthrough and final walkthrough, but you don't need to be present during the actual painting. We're professional, respectful, and can work independently.

Preparation & Cleanup

How well do you protect the surroundings while the project is in progress?

We're known as "the neatest painters in Los Angeles" - we have the most shop vacs in town!

Our protection process includes:

  • Covering all furniture with drop cloths
  • Protecting floors with canvas or plastic
  • Masking trim, windows, and hardware
  • Removing and covering outlet/switch plates
  • Protecting landscaping for exterior work

After completion, all areas are vacuumed and moved items are replaced carefully.
